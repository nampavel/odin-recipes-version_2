Garden Salad

Description

Garden salad for 8 servings within 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 head green leaf lettuce
  • 1 english cucumber
  • 4 radishes
  • 3 green onions

Steps

  1. Whisk heavy cream and seasonings, set aside
  2. Slice lettuce, cucumber, radishes and green onions in a bowl. Add the dressing.
